ABOUT OUR EVENT

Crypto 4 Your Thoughts (C4YT) is a Blockchain hackathon focused on fostering a strong, diverse open source community working together to build more accessible tools within the cryptocurrency ecosystem. C4YT will bring together 300 top engineers in Cryptocurrency, JavaScript/NodeJs communities, high-profile sponsors, speakers, and recruiters.
